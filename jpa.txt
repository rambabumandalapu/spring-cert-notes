limit – result of the query can be limited by usage of first/top keyword

ordering operator – optionally you can specify ordering operator at the end of method name

findByLastnameOrderByFirstnameDesc



Note:
The findTopByOrderByAgeDesc method does not require a filtering property after By. While most finder methods use By followed by a property (like findByLastname), in this case, findTopBy simply means get the first result after applying the OrderBy clause. It’s valid syntax, even without a filtering condition.
